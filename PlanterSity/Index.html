How do you ensure the safety of plants during delivery?

We take great care in packaging our plants to ensure they arrive in perfect condition. Each plant is securely placed in a transparent package with ample cushioning to prevent damage during transit. We also provide air holes in the packaging to ensure proper ventilation and keep the plant healthy during the journey.

Do I need special lighting for my indoor plants?

While some plants thrive in natural light, others may require additional lighting, especially during the winter months. Grow lights can be a great solution to ensure your plants receive adequate light for healthy growth.

How do I prevent pests on my indoor plants?

To prevent pests, regularly inspect your plants for signs of infestation, such as discolored leaves or webbing. Keep the plants clean by wiping the leaves with a damp cloth and ensure proper air circulation. If pests are detected, natural remedies like neem oil or insecticidal soap can be used.

What should I do if my plant arrives damaged?

Although we take every precaution to ensure safe delivery, if your plant arrives damaged, please contact our customer service team within 48 hours of receiving your order. We will be happy to assist you with a replacement or refund.

How do you handle extreme weather conditions during delivery

We closely monitor weather conditions and may delay shipments during extreme temperatures to protect the plants. If a delay is necessary, we will notify you and provide updates on when your plant will be safely delivered.
